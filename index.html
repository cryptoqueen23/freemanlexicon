Why historical dictionaries?

Language changes over time. Modern dictionaries often update definitions to reflect current usage. Freeman Lexicon preserves historical editions so readers can understand words in the context they carried when older laws, contracts, and court opinions were written.

Help Preserve Public Knowledge

Freeman Lexicon is free, contains no ads, and is built entirely from public-domain sources. Donations help fund hosting, indexing, and new historical collections.
